About Me

After two years working in finance, first in traditional wealth management for a financial advisor and then for a cryptocurrency investment firm, I transitioned into becoming a software engineer. I took a full stack 16 week bootcamp at App Academy's NYC campus. There, I learned Ruby, Javascript, SQL, HTML, CSS, Ruby on Rails, React (w/Redux), Node.js, Postgres, MongoDB, Express, Git and AWS storage to name a few. I am interested in blockchain, market design, payment/trust networks, and novel financial tools.
